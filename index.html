📖 history

Once upon a time, there was a little boy named Sami, who lived in Eritrea. Every day, he went to the park with his dog Kelbi to play. There, he met his friends, picked flowers, and learned new words in Tigrinya. ቐይሕ ዕንባባ (Qeyh Abeba) means "red flower". Sami loved listening to stories and discovering new things!

Privacy Policy

Data Collection: This website does not collect, process, or store any personal data on a server. All user interactions, settings, and preferences are handled entirely within your browser and remain on your device.

Local Storage: Any data such as audio preferences, selected language, or usage statistics is saved locally in your browser's memory (e.g. localStorage or sessionStorage) and is not shared with any third-party service.

No Cookies / No Tracking: This site does not use tracking cookies, analytics tools, or any third-party services that monitor your behavior. Your activity is private and inaccessible to anyone but you.

Notifications and Profile Visibility: The notification and profile visibility options only affect what is shown on your screen. These features do not transmit or store any data externally.

Audio Output: The audio feature is generated on your device and does not require an internet connection or backend processing. Your audio settings are used solely to personalize your experience.

Children’s Privacy: This website is designed with children in mind and avoids collecting any identifiable or sensitive information. Parents can rest assured that the app operates safely offline.

Your Control: You can clear your browser data at any time to reset all settings and remove any stored preferences or statistics.

Contact: If you have any questions about privacy, feel free to contact the developer (if contact information is available on the homepage or about section.)
